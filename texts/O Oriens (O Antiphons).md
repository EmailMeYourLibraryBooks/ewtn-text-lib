# O Oriens (O Antiphons)

***

## Setting: Traditional plainchant

O Oriens,
splendor lucis aeternae, 
et sol justitiae:
veni, et illumina 
sedentes in tenebris, 
et umbra mortis.

O Rising Dawn,
radiance of eternal light
and Sun of Justice:
Come, enlighten those
sitting in darkness 
and in the shadow of death.
